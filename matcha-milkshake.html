Iâ€™m showing you how to make an easy matcha milkshake with either vanilla ice cream or my homemade matcha ice cream. Both versions are easy and incredible!

Ingredients

  • Matcha powder
  • Milk
  • Vanilla ice cream OR matcha green tea ice cream

Instructions

  1. Whisk together the matcha powder and 3 tablespoons of milk to remove all clumps. Add to blender
  2. Add 3 scoops of ice cream to blender and blend until incorporated.
